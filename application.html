Application

The Early College Studies Program at Stamford High School will be accepting up to 75 students into the incoming 2025-2026 freshman class. All of these slots will be allocated on a lottery based application process. Students not accepted via the lottery will be put on an ordered waitlist.

Timeline:

  • Application Opens 11/7/2024
  • Application Due 1/15/2025
  • Lottery Held 2/18/2025
  • Acceptance of position needs to be in by 3/11/2025. If position is not confirmed by this date, it will be offered to those on the ordered waitlist.

Requirements

  • Applicants must be residents of the City of Stamford.
  • Even if you are zoned to a different school like Westhill, you can still apply. Note that you would have to apply to and attend Stamford High if accepted.
